Our School History

Our history began with a small church in Pajangan City. Starting from the rapid growth of the Christian community, thus encouraging a high need to receive Christian education. Along with that, our Pastor, Pastor Venerah Gabriel proposed the construction of a Christian school, so that students can be educated in Christian teachings and grow in knowing God. In 1990, our school began construction and was completed in 1995. Until 2023 yesterday, we just finished renovating the school building, so that it provides more comfort for students. It is recorded that until 2025 there are 1500 students from kindergarten to high school levels.

Our Vision

To become a leading Christian school that forms a generation with Christ-like character and a global outlook. Our school is committed to developing the academic and spiritual potential of each student. Through education centered on love and faith, we prepare students to become future leaders.

Our Mission

Our teachers guide students toward truth, knowledge, and growth in faith. With love, they help students discover their God-given potential. Our teachers create a safe, supportive, Christ-centered learning environment. They build partnerships with parents to ensure students grow academically and spiritually. Through their lifelong role models, our teachers prepare students to be faithful leaders who impact the world.
