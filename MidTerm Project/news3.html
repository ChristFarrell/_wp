Our News and Information

Sola Gratia School

Another proud achievement was achieved by Sola Gratia students in the national Science Fair held in Jakarta on April 5-7, 2025. In the competition which was attended by more than 80 schools from all over Indonesia, the Sola Gratia team won 1st place in the Science Innovation Project category and 2nd place in the Scientific Poster category. The main team consisting of three grade XI students presented a project entitled "Eco-Cooler: Environmentally Friendly Cooler from Used Materials". This project combines the principles of physics and the environment to create an air conditioner without electricity, using only used plastic bottles and cardboard. "I didn't expect to win at the national level. Initially, we just wanted to try our simple idea," said Reza, the team leader, when interviewed after the announcement of the winners. The supervising teacher, Mrs. Diah, added, "These children are extraordinary. They worked hard for weeks and learned a lot about scientific research firsthand." In addition to bringing home trophies and certificates, students also received invitations to participate in advanced coaching programs from partner universities. The Principal of Sola Gratia, Mrs. Maria Lestari, expressed her pride and gratitude for this achievement. "This achievement is proof that our students' creativity and hard work can compete at the national level," she said. This achievement is an inspiration for other students to continue to work and not be afraid to try new things. Sola Gratia will continue to support students' interests and talents in various fields, including science, art, and technology.
