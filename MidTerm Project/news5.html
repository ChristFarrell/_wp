Our News and Information

Sola Gratia School

In an effort to improve the quality of education, Sola Gratia established an official collaboration with the Regional Government on Friday, May 2, 2025. The signing of the memorandum of understanding (MoU) took place in the Sola Gratia Hall and was attended by the Head of the local Education Office, school leaders, and representatives of teachers and students. This collaboration includes improving learning facilities, teacher training, and character education programs that are in line with Sola Gratia's vision and mission as an educational institution based on Christian values. The Principal of Sola Gratia, Mrs. Maria Lestari, expressed her gratitude for the support provided by the government. "We believe that this collaboration will be an important step in forming a young generation that excels academically and morally," she said. Meanwhile, Mr. Andi Prasetya as the Head of the Education Office added, "The government strongly supports initiatives like this. Private and public schools must work side by side for the future of our children." This collaboration will be realized in the form of regular training, educational visits, and assistance with digital learning equipment. It is hoped that this collaboration will be a positive example for other schools in the area. With the spirit of togetherness, Sola Gratia continues to demonstrate its commitment to educating students holistically and with high competitiveness.
