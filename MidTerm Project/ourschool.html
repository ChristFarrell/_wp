Our School

Drs. Kevin Handreas Widjaja

Director of Sola Gratia School

Welcome to Sola Gratia School. We believe that the best education is born from professionalism, dedication, and a clear vision. Therefore, we present competent and experienced teachers, who not only teach, but also guide with their hearts. Our curriculum is designed comprehensively to equip students with knowledge, 21st century skills, and strong character. The learning process is strengthened by complete facilities such as comfortable classrooms, laboratories, digital libraries, and other supporting facilities. We understand that every child is a unique individual who must be prepared for a dynamic future. Through collaboration between schools, teachers, and parents, we build a safe, positive, and inspiring learning environment. Thank you for your trust. Let's grow together at Sola Gratia School, the birthplace of superior and integrity generations.
